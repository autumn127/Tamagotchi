# Tamagotchi
Autumn Lee ID: 69717987, Kevin Nguyen ID: 65512368

The hamster will roam around the screen by himself.  
Hearts will decrease by one every hour.
Steps will decrease by 100 every hour.
Tap him to make him laugh.
Tap the seed icon to feed him and restore hearts.
If overfed (has 10 full hearts), he will flop over and refuse to eat.
Tap the steps icon to start the pedometer, and he will begin running. The app must be running.
Tap it again to turn off the pedometer.
Tap the stamp icon to write a letter (tap it again to close).
